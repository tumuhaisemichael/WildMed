Medical Program Types

Comprehensive healthcare programs that combine medical expertise with community outreach and safari operations

Mobile Clinics

Deliver essential healthcare services to remote rural communities using fully equipped mobile medical units.

Duration: 2-4 weeks | Requirements: MD/RN

Medical Training

Train local healthcare workers in advanced medical techniques and emergency response protocols.

Duration: 3-6 weeks | Requirements: Specialist MD

Emergency Support

Provide emergency medical support for safari operations and wilderness expeditions.

Duration: 1-3 weeks | Requirements: Emergency MD

Remote Healthcare

Deliver healthcare services to the most isolated communities in challenging wilderness environments.

Duration: 2-5 weeks | Requirements: General Practice

Medical Specialties Required

Healthcare expertise needed for our medical expedition programs

Emergency Medicine

Critical care and trauma management in remote settings

Requirements:
  • • Board certification in Emergency Medicine
  • • Minimum 3 years experience
  • • Wilderness medicine training preferred
Commitment: 2-4 weeks

General Practice

Primary care and preventive medicine for communities

Requirements:
  • • MD or equivalent medical degree
  • • Family medicine or internal medicine
  • • Experience with tropical diseases
Commitment: 3-6 weeks

Public Health

Community health programs and disease prevention

Requirements:
  • • MPH or equivalent public health degree
  • • Community health experience
  • • Program development skills
Commitment: 4-8 weeks

Tropical Medicine

Specialized care for tropical and infectious diseases

Requirements:
  • • Tropical medicine certification
  • • Infectious disease experience
  • • Research background preferred
Commitment: 3-5 weeks

Surgery

Essential surgical procedures in field conditions

Requirements:
  • • Board certification in surgery
  • • General surgery experience
  • • Field surgery training preferred
Commitment: 2-3 weeks

Nursing

Comprehensive nursing care and patient education

Requirements:
  • • RN license and BSN degree
  • • Minimum 2 years clinical experience
  • • Emergency nursing preferred
Commitment: 2-6 weeks
