Best Wildlife Viewing Seasons

Dry Season (June - September)

  • Best time for gorilla trekking with drier trails
  • Wildlife gathers around water sources in Akagera
  • Clear skies and minimal rainfall

Wet Season (October - May)

  • Lush green landscapes and dramatic skies
  • Fewer tourists and lower prices
  • Birdwatching at its best with migratory species

Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Rwanda adventure

Visa Requirements

  • Visa on arrival for most nationalities
  • East African Tourist Visa available (covers Rwanda, Kenya, Uganda)
  • Valid for 30 days
  • Passport valid 6+ months

Health & Safety

  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate required
  • Malaria prophylaxis recommended
  • Travel insurance essential
  • One of Africa's safest countries

Packing Essentials

  • Waterproof hiking boots for gorilla trekking
  • Rain jacket (weather can be unpredictable)
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Insect repellent

Climate

  • Temperate tropical highland climate
  • Dry seasons: June-September and December-February
  • Rainy seasons: March-May and October-November
  • Temperature: 15-27°C (cooler in mountainous areas)

Currency & Payments

  • Rwandan Franc (RWF)
  • USD widely accepted in tourist areas
  • Credit cards accepted in major hotels and restaurants
  • ATMs available in cities and towns

Language & Culture

  • Kinyarwanda, French, and English official languages
  • English widely spoken in tourist areas
  • Friendly, welcoming people
  • Rich cultural heritage with traditional dance and art
