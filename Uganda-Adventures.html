Best Wildlife Viewing Seasons

Dry Season (June - September)

  • Best for gorilla and chimp trekking
  • Animals gather at water sources
  • Minimal rainfall, easier hiking

Wet Season (October - May)

  • Lush green landscapes
  • Fewer tourists, lower prices
  • Bird watching at its best

Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Uganda adventure

Visa Requirements

  • eVisa available online
  • $50 single entry visa
  • Valid for 90 days
  • Passport valid 6+ months

Health & Safety

  • Yellow fever vaccination required
  • Malaria prophylaxis recommended
  • Travel insurance essential
  • Generally safe for tourists

Packing Essentials

  • Waterproof hiking boots
  • Rain jacket and layers
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Insect repellent

Climate

  • Tropical climate year-round
  • Dry seasons: Jun-Sep & Dec-Feb
  • Wet seasons: Mar-May & Oct-Nov
  • Temperature: 17-29°C

Currency & Payments

  • Ugandan Shilling (UGX)
  • USD widely accepted
  • Credit cards in major hotels
  • ATMs available in cities

Language & Culture

  • English official language
  • Swahili widely spoken
  • Friendly, welcoming people
  • Rich cultural heritage
