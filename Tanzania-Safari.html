Best Wildlife Viewing Seasons

Dry Season (June - October)

  • Excellent wildlife viewing with animals gathering at water sources
  • Great Migration river crossings in northern Serengeti
  • Clear skies and minimal rainfall

Green Season (November - May)

  • Calving season with thousands of newborn animals
  • Lush landscapes and dramatic skies
  • Fewer crowds and lower prices

Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Tanzania adventure

Visa Requirements

  • Tourist visa required for most nationalities
  • Available on arrival or online
  • Valid for 90 days
  • Passport valid 6+ months

Health & Safety

  • Yellow fever vaccination required
  • Malaria prophylaxis recommended
  • Travel insurance essential
  • Generally safe for tourists

Packing Essentials

  • Neutral colored clothing
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Insect repellent

Climate

  • Tropical climate year-round
  • Dry season: June-October
  • Wet season: November-May
  • Temperature: 20-30°C

Currency & Payments

  • Tanzanian Shilling (TZS)
  • USD widely accepted
  • Credit cards in major hotels
  • ATMs available in cities

Language & Culture

  • Swahili and English official
  • English widely spoken
  • Friendly, welcoming people
  • Rich cultural heritage
