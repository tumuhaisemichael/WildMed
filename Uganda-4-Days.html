Day 1: Journey to Murchison Falls

Northwest Uganda

Travel Highlights

  • • Stop at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary
  • • Nile River viewpoints
  • • Lodge check-in and orientation

Safari Preparation

  • • Game drive briefing
  • • Camera equipment check
  • • Binocular distribution
  • • Evening wildlife presentation
2

Day 2: Murchison Falls Safari

Murchison Falls NP

Morning Activities

  • • Dawn game drive in savanna
  • • Lion, elephant, and giraffe spotting
  • • Birdwatching (450+ species)
  • • Bush breakfast experience

Afternoon Highlights

  • • Boat cruise to the base of the falls
  • • Hippo and crocodile viewing
  • • Hike to top of Murchison Falls
  • • Sunset photography session

Wildlife Insight

Murchison Falls is home to 76 mammal species and 450 bird species. The Nile's dramatic constriction creates one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.

Kibale Forest has the highest concentration of primates in Africa, with over 1,450 chimpanzees in the park's 795 km² area.
