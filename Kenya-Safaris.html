Best Wildlife Viewing Seasons

Dry Season (June - October)

  • Peak wildlife viewing with animals gathering at water sources
  • Great Migration in Maasai Mara (July-October)
  • Clear skies and minimal rainfall

Green Season (November - May)

  • Calving season with thousands of newborn animals
  • Lush landscapes and dramatic skies
  • Fewer crowds and lower prices

Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Kenya adventure

Visa Requirements

  • Tourist visa required for most nationalities
  • Available online (e-Visa) before travel
  • Valid for 90 days
  • Passport valid 6+ months

Health & Safety

  • Yellow fever vaccination required if coming from endemic area
  • Malaria prophylaxis recommended
  • Travel insurance essential
  • Generally safe for tourists

Packing Essentials

  • Neutral colored clothing (khaki, green, beige)
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Insect repellent

Climate

  • Tropical climate with warm days and cool nights
  • Dry season: June-October (coolest months)
  • Wet seasons: March-May (long rains) and Nov-Dec (short rains)
  • Temperature: 20-28°C (varies by altitude)

Currency & Payments

  • Kenyan Shilling (KES)
  • USD widely accepted in tourist areas
  • Credit cards accepted in major hotels and lodges
  • ATMs widely available in cities and towns

Language & Culture

  • Swahili and English official languages
  • English widely spoken in tourist areas
  • Friendly, welcoming people
  • Rich cultural heritage with 40+ ethnic groups
